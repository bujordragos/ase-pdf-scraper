╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

📥 QUICK START:
  1. Double-click ASE_PDF_Scraper.exe
  2. Select your faculty, program, and year from the dropdowns
  3. Click "Download Subject PDFs"
  4. Find your PDFs in the ASE_PDFs folder

✨ FEATURES:
  • Downloads ALL obligatory subjects for your year
  • Organizes by semester (Semestrul I / Semestrul II)
  • Clean filenames without special characters
  • Remembers your last settings
  • Works for all ASE faculties

📁 OUTPUT STRUCTURE:
  ASE_PDFs/
    └── Program_Year_Period/
        ├── Semestrul_I/
        │   ├── Econometrie.pdf
        │   ├── Statistica.pdf
        │   └── ...
        └── Semestrul_II/
            └── ...

🔄 UPDATING:
  • Old downloads automatically move to _archive/ folder
  • You can compare old vs new versions
  • Re-run anytime to get fresh copies

⚠️ IMPORTANT:
  • Requires internet connection
  • Only downloads "obligatory" (O) subjects
  • Downloads take 2-3 minutes (be patient!)

💡 TIPS:
  • Cancel button works - stops mid-download if needed
  • Check _logs/ folder if something goes wrong
  • Settings are saved between runs
